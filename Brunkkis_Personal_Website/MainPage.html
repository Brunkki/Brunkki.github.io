COOl programs you should try:

  1. 1.DriveDX


This is an absolute must-have for macs with aging hard drives. It checks the S.M.A.R.T data of your drive and looks for errors, informing you of a dying hard drive before it bites the dust, so you don’t have to lose any data.


Download DriveDX here: https://binaryfruit.com/drivedx



  1. 2.Discord


You probably know of this already, but if you don’t, then this is an absolutely awesome messaging platform, and my go-to for quick messaging. It functions similarly to Slack, but has a friendlier user-interface, and a cute mascot called Wumpus.

You can create and join servers, instant message people, and even create your own emoji.


Download Discord here: https://discord.com/download



  1. 3. Dosdude1 macOS Patcher


Is your mac getting old, it hasn’t had featrue updates in a few years, and you’re stuck on an older version of macOS? Don’t worry. Dosdude1’s awesome patcher tools will help you get your mac up to date, it’s almost as easy as updating an actually supported mac.

The way it works is that it stops Apple from checking if your computer is compatible with the OS you are installing.
